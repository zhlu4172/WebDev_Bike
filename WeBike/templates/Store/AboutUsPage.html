Our Goal

To foster a community around the emerging e-bike industry in all the main cities within Australia.

Rafa's Story

Having lost his job working at a resuturant during the pandemic, Rafael started selling and fixing bikes with his dad in order to maintain a source of income. Being an international student himself, Rafael understood the troubles of navigating an unfamiliar area and the cost that would come with it.

It was during this time that Rafael realised the potential of a marketplace platform to buy, sell and rent e-bikes, to foster an e-bike community that would allow visitors and locals to travel in a cheap, easy and fun way.
Just like all good startups, WeBike began in his garage, and ever since then Rafael has been committed to turning his dream into a reality.
